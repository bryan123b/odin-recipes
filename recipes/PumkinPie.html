Alton Brown Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Description

A traditional pumpkin pie serving 8 people

Ingredients

  • gingersnap cookies
  • dark brown sugar
  • ground ginger
  • unsalted butter, melted
  • can pumpkin puree
  • half and half
  • freshly grated nutmeg
  • kosher salt
  • large eggs
  • large egg yolk
